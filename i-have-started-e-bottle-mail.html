  • 手元から離れたら気配は消えます（PV数やいいね数のノイズは見えません）
    Once it leaves your hands, its presence vanishes (you won't see the noise of view counts or "likes").

  • 広大なネットの海を漂い、どこかの誰かに静かに拾われるのを待ちます。
    It drifts across the vast ocean of the internet, waiting to be quietly picked up by someone, somewhere.

    Only when a reaction arrives—perhaps just as you’ve forgotten about it—can you finally confirm that it actually reached someone.
